2026

NLP: Toxic Comment Classification (Jigsaw Dataset) Jan 2026

Implemented and compared two NLP modelling paradigms for toxic comment classification using the Jigsaw dataset: a statistical model (TF-IDF + Logistic Regression) and a neural contextual model (DistilBERT). The project explored how different modelling approaches capture harmful language patterns, evaluating both predictive performance and interpretability.

2025

Privacy Engineering: Privacy Impact Assessment of Docspell Dec 2025

Conducted a Privacy Impact Assessment (PIA) of the open-source document management system Docspell using Data Flow Diagrams and the LINDDUN threat modeling framework. Identified potential privacy risks and proposed privacy-by-design mitigation strategies.

Research Publication: Web & Social Media Archiving Jul 2025

Co-authored the research article "The evolving landscape of web and social media archiving". The study investigates how web and social media archiving practices have evolved across 17 institutions in Europe and North America in response to both the COVID-19 pandemic and the growing influence of AI-driven technologies.

JCO Company Website Redesign May 2025

Led the strategic redesign of the JCO company website to improve event management workflow and user journey. The redesign improved data collection for event-related sales and increased engagement, with the most viewed article exceeding 450,000 views.

Python: Exploratory Analysis on Novel Word Rating Datasets Jan 2023

Exploratory data analysis using Pandas, SciPy, Seaborn, and Matplotlib to test hypotheses and analyze correlations within novel word rating datasets.
